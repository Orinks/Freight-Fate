- leg: cheyenne_wy_us -> salt_lake_city_ut_us
- at_mi: 230
- spoken: Billboard: Little America is ahead. It offers cheap fuel, famous ice cream cones, a stuffed penguin, and a sign every mile reminding you that it is still ahead. That is how traditions survive in the middle of nowhere.

- spoken: Ahead, the highway climbs Donner Pass high in the Sierra Nevada. A wagon party bound for California was trapped here by deep snow in the winter of eighteen forty-six and survived only by the grimmest of means. It is beautiful country with a hard history.

### Pony Express Station
- treatment: billboard
- leg: omaha_ne_us -> cheyenne_wy_us
- at_mi: 250
- spoken: Billboard: An original Pony Express station is ahead in Gothenburg. Teenage riders once swapped horses here at a gallop while carrying the mail west.
