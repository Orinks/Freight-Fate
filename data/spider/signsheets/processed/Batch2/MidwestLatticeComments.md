# MidwestLattice — design comments and 1.9 ideas

These notes are optional game-layer ideas for Claude and the Freight Fate team. They are not bake input. Existing and newly added locations may support pull-offs, achievements, souvenirs, audio scenes, buffs, or future mini-games.

## General review
- Billboard density should stay uneven enough to feel regional rather than procedural.
- Prioritize distinctive audio hooks: unusual names, food rituals, giant objects, local boasts, and signs that become funnier across repeated trips.
- Any entry carrying a `****` note needs human review before baking.
